Core Functions

Aggregate

Pull all your Cyber Threat Intelligence into one endpoint.

Search

Search through your feeds at once.

Score

Cross the data and attribute a "how much malicious" mark.

And much more...

Integrable

Thanks to its REST-API, integrate the power of hippocampe in your project.

Web Front-End

Bored of colorless command line ? A graphical user interface is available through http.

Feeds Monitoring

Follow your feeds' life: which failed to be indexed and how many new indicators per day ?

Periodic Update

Schedule automatic indexation in a cron syntax style and keep being up to date.

Data Visualization

Identify visually which feed contributes the most and which observable type is recurrent.
